About Doclinic

Doclinic is part of the Multimedia Biometric Health Assessment System, designed to provide a comprehensive overview of your health. The platform collects real-time biometric data using advanced sensors and provides detailed reports on key health indicators.

The system focuses on monitoring and reporting on critical health metrics such as:

  • Diabetes Check – Measures glucose levels and flags any abnormalities.
  • Blood Pressure (BP) Monitoring – Tracks blood pressure levels and provides insights into hypertension risks.
  • Cardiovascular Health – Monitors heart rate, ECG, and other cardio-related metrics.
  • Neurological Health – Analyzes brain activity, stress levels, and other neurological indicators.

Through interactive, easy-to-read reports, Doclinic helps both users and healthcare professionals identify potential health risks and take timely action. Whether for personal use or in a medical setting, our platform is designed to promote better health management.

By combining accurate biometric data collection with detailed reports, Doclinic empowers users with the knowledge they need to make informed health decisions.
